Bio

I currently work at work at Nachi-Fujikoshi Corp.In March 2021 I graduated from Waseda University where I studied specializing in Robotics. I was advised by Professor Shigeki Sugano and co-advised by Associate Professor Mitsuhiro Kamezaki. My research was abput design and development of new type of Soft Tactile sensors for robots. Apart from this, I also worked on various other topics which you can find below.

Buring my time at Waseda I also worked as a Research Assistant at Sony CSL on the Superception research framework. Before that, I was at Telexistense Inc working on their flagship robot (Model T) to develop next-generation robotic end effectors. In the past, I worked at Whill Inc on robot localization and path planning for the intelligent personal EV (Electric Vehicle) Model C. I have also briefly worked as an application development associate at Accenture Technologies Pvt Ltd.
