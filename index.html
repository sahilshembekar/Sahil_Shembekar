Hey!

I am Sahil. I am a Graduate student at Waseda University specializing in Robotics and, I also work as a Research Assistant at Sony CSL on the Superception research framework. At Waseda, I am advised by Professor Shigeki Sugano and co-advised by Associate Professor Mitsuhiro Kamezaki.

Before this, I was at Telexistense Inc working on their flagship robot (Model T) to develop next-generation robotic end effectors. In the past, I worked at Whill Inc on robot localization and path planning for the intelligent personal EV (Electric Vehicle) Model C. I have also briefly worked as an application development associate at Accenture Technologies Pvt Ltd.

My research is in the field of robot end effectors, robot actuation systems, and tactile sensors with the focus on making robots more safe, versatile, and intelligent. I work on mechanical design, electronic design, simulation, and sensor-robot integration associated with the tactile sensors and robots.
